About Recipe Wala

Our Story ...


This is a simple app that intends to show how AngularJs, Node, MongoDb and ElasticSearch play together. Here we have a very large JSON file that has lots and lots of recipes stored in them. We will use a type-ahead to search for different recipes. Also people will be able to login and add their own recipes and share their secret with rest of the world.

This application intends to provide the hidden chefs to come out and show their cutlery skills to the entire world.
